How does the app work?

Optimized for minimal internet usage and privacy, featuring:

  • 1. Multi-Database Barcode Scanner Searches multiple global databases (Open Food Facts, USDA, UPCitemdb), saves products offline, and automatically highlights gluten ingredients.
  • 2. Smart Camera Text Scan (Local AI) Point your camera at ingredients. Uses a free, on-device AI engine (0 cost, highly private) to read text and highlight allergens.
