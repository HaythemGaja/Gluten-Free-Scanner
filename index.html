How does the app work?

Optimized for minimal internet usage and privacy (No AI), featuring:

  • 1. Offline Ingredient Analysis Copy ingredients using your phone's native camera text scanner and paste them here for instant local analysis.
  • 2. Cached Barcode Scanner Searches the Open Food Facts database and saves scanned products for offline use later.
  • 3. Fast & Accurate Results A rule-based algorithm instead of AI ensures blazing fast, private results.
